Introduction

This website provides a tool for calculating Intraocular Lens (IOL) power ("Tool") designed to assist eye care professionals in selecting the appropriate IOL for patients undergoing cataract surgery. The Tool is intended as an adjunctive resource to complement, not replace, the comprehensive ophthalmic examination and diagnostic testing traditionally employed in clinical practice. The results obtained by the calculator are not intended to serve as medical or surgical instruction.

Accuracy and Limitations

The results generated by the Tool are based on mathematical models and should be used in conjunction with clinical judgement and professional standards of care. While we strive for accuracy, the variability in patient anatomies, surgical techniques, and measurement devices means that the Tool's predictions are not guaranteed. The responsibility for the final IOL selection rests with the treating physician, and users should verify calculations through alternative means where possible.
